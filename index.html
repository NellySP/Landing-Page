About us

Here at Leafly we are on a mission… We aim to offer our customers the most sustainable and high quality plants, grown locally in our own greenhouse. We’ve been on this mission since April 2021, when our shop were finally able to open up! Our focus is on making good plants and plant products affordable for everybody, from experts to beginners.
